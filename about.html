We build digital presence for businesses that deserve to be found online

OnlineChalo is a Mumbai-based web design and development company. We're small by choice — so every project gets our full attention.

Our story

OnlineChalo was born from a simple observation: thousands of great businesses in Mumbai and across India are invisible on the internet. The clinic with a brilliant doctor, the coaching class with amazing results, the bakery with the best cakes in town — they're all losing customers because they don't show up when someone searches Google.

We decided to fix that. Not as a giant agency with 50-person teams and corporate overheads. But as a focused team that builds fast, delivers quality, and stays honest about pricing.

We come from an IT training background — we run a computer education institute where we teach everything from Tally to web development. That experience gives us a unique advantage: we understand technology deeply, and we know how to explain it simply. We speak your language, not tech jargon.

Why we're different

🏢 We have a real office

Visit us in Sion West, Mumbai. Sit across the table from us. Discuss your project face to face. In a world where freelancers work from coffee shops and disappear after payment, our office is our accountability.

⚙️ We build our own products

We're not just service providers — we've built Rememo, a full SaaS platform for bakery management, from the ground up. We know what it takes to build software that works in the real world.

📢 We practice what we preach

We run Google Ads for our own institute. We've optimized our own Google Business Profile. When we advise you, it's from experience — not textbooks.

🤝 We don't overpromise

If your budget isn't enough for what you need, we'll tell you. If a project doesn't make sense, we'll say so. We'd rather earn your trust than your money.
